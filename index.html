swiss ski card

One skipass for all ski areas in Switzerland

Never seen before in Switzerland: With the Swiss Ski Card, up to 87 ski resorts with a total of up to 2,815 varied kilometers of slopes are open to you - and that from the start in November to sunny skiing in April! With more than 900 state-of-the-art cable cars and lifts, you can quickly reach the starting points of your skiing adventure, and around 800 huts and mountain restaurants invite you to stop for a refreshment break. The Swiss Ski Card region is unique in its size and variety of offers: discover the endless possibilities on skis or snowboard, from glacier ski areas to the longest valley runs. No less than 87 ski resorts offer plenty of variety and always the perfect offer for your wishes and needs.
