do you have any questions?

Essentially, there is no replacement for forgotten or lost Swiss Ski Card. However, holders of Swiss Ski Card have the option to request a new ticket at a cash desk in the ski area. This process involves paying a handling fee and a deposit for the new KeyCard, and it requires presenting a document and order confirmation along with a valid ID.

Discounts are offered to children, young individuals and seniors with the ski pass.

After falling ill or sustaining an injury, kindly reach out to the company where you acquired the card promptly. While there is no legal entitlement to goodwill reimbursement, it will be assessed upon request. Please ensure the submission of a medical certificate.

You can pay with various methods, including credit cards, debit cards, PayPal and electronic transfers.

When ordering online, the Swiss Ski Card will be dispatched within two working days. As a rule, delivery takes at least three working days after ordering.
